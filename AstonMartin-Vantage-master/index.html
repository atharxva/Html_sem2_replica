A born predator

The new Vantage displays the most marked and provocative design ever seen on a Vantage. It's a hunter as light in physical terms as it is visually.

Exterior

The Vantage masterfully and uncompromisingly demonstrates all of Aston Martin's principles of purity and perfect proportions. Although it shows noticeable changes, due to its significant advances, it still reflects its magnificent pedigree. Since its badge was launched in the 1950s as an upgrade from the DB2, the Vantage has been synonymous with performance, becoming the icon of the Aston Martin brand.

Interior

The cockpit impression conveyed by the interior expresses an absolute concentration and functionality. The lowered driving position offers a more immersive experience and increases the intimacy of the link between the driver, the car, and the road.

Sporting elegance and agility

The Vantage is incredibly fast, but this speed does not in any way overshadow the sensations and pleasure. It's a car dedicated to the driver.

Remarkable sporting agility

Our talented engineers wanted ideal foundations for the new Vantage. That's why the chassis is made of lightweight bonded aluminum. Its compact dimensions, an impressive weight of only 1,530 kg*, and an almost perfect weight distribution of 50:50 give the Vantage its inherent sporting agility. Within this structure, the rigid subframe provides increased stability for the suspension, with the main advantage being wonderfully responsive control behind the wheel. Car and driver are in symbiosis for unparalleled dynamic and exhilarating driving.
